Blackjack Rules

How to play?

  • Select the bet bet-amount you want to bet.
  • Click Hit to draw card.
  • Try to get as close to 21 without going over.
  • Once decided, click Stand to let dealer play.
  • Dealer will play only after player clicks stand.
  • If the player wins the round, 2X 'Current Bet' is added to Money.
  • If the player loses the round, the 'Current Bet' amount is lost

Card values

  • Card values and total are shown during play.
  • Ace can be 1 or 11. Face cards are 10.
  • If you go over 21, you bust and lose the round.
  • If both hands tie, it's a push and your bet is returned.
Note: Any progress in the game will not be saved.
